Foreword

I made the decision to enroll at UIU for the 212 batch, but I must admit that I haven't been actively engaged in my studies or campus life. My lack of interest led me to frequently skip classes, and I didn't bother joining any varsity clubs either. To my astonishment, I woke up one day to realize that six semesters had already flown by, and my CGPA was disappointingly low. Now, I find myself in the midst of the 7th-semester midterms, and my prolonged indifference has resulted in a terrible performance in this exam. I fully understand the urgency of the situation and the need to turn my academic career around, including salvaging this semester. I am committed to making a positive change and taking proactive steps to improve my performance throughout my time at the university.
